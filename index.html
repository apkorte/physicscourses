Physics Revision — Common Questions

An A-level Physics revision course is an intensive online programme designed for Physics exam preparation — consolidating syllabus understanding and building exam technique ahead of A-level examinations. Dr Korte's courses cover the full content in small groups, with targeted support for AQA, OCR, Edexcel and WJEC Eduqas students.

Courses cover all major UK A-level Physics exam boards: AQA, OCR A, OCR B (Advancing Physics), Edexcel, and WJEC Eduqas.

Sessions run live via Zoom in small groups. Dr Korte teaches using shared digital materials, works through problems in real time, and tailors explanations to the questions that arise. Students need only a device with internet access.

Easter intensive courses run during the Easter school holiday period in April — ideal for final exam preparation before the summer exams. Christmas intensive courses run between Christmas and New Year, giving Year 12 students a head start on their second term and Year 13 students early exam preparation. Summer intensive courses run in August ahead of resit examinations or Year 12 progression. Exact dates and booking are at physicsandmathscourses.com.
